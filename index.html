Thrifiting & Reselling Online

The Poshmark App & Ebay

Poshmark is an App used to buy and sell primarily used clothing and accessories. I came accross this app just browsing online in 2017. I didn't have much interest in the app at first but then while researching it, came accross posts by peopoe shopping at thrift stores and flipping higher-end/trendy or vintage items on the app. I have alway LOVED thrifing and started hunting for different items that were popular on the app in my local thirft stores. I made my first sale after finding a pair of lululemon leggings for $3 at Savers and selling them for $25 in one day! I later found out they were very underpriced but still, I was hooked!

Hobby to Small Buisness

There is a huge demand for gently used clothing of specific brands or trends online. Buyers get a huge discounted price and price negotiating adds a benefit for both buyers and sellers not usually avaialable in traditional retail stores. Thrift stores sell items at a very low cost and many items sell for around %50 of the retail price. Turning a profit is easy on in demand items in good condition. I started shopping for items at local thirft stores and listing them for sale on the Poshmark App and Ebay regularly. I was able to make enough money to significantly cut down on my hours at my job and spend more time at home with children. I realized I needed to research how to actually make this hobby into a buisness and contacted a CPA for assistance with taxes and getting a buisness license.

Bonus, It's Good for The Environment

According to the EPA's website in 2015 there were 10.5 million tons of textiles added to landfills. It's also noted that the recycle rate for clothing and shoes is estimated at %14.2 according to this Newsweek article The fast fashion industry has increased the amount and rate of clothing sent to landfills. Reselling is recycling, so there is an added benefit of helping the environment!
